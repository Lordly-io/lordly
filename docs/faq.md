# FAQ

## What is lOrdly?

lOrdly is a Decision Intelligence platform for digital businesses. We help companies discover where they're losing sales and which action to prioritize — in plain language, not charts.

## Is the platform available?

Yes. The platform is available at [app.lordly.io](https://app.lordly.io).

There is also a free ChatAI focused on marketing and business growth at [chat.lordly.io](https://chat.lordly.io).

## Who is lOrdly for?

CEOs, directors, and growth managers of digital businesses and e-commerces looking to make faster, data-backed decisions — without needing a data analyst.

## How is lOrdly different from a BI tool or dashboard?

Dashboards show what happened. lOrdly tells you why it happened, how much it's costing, and exactly what to do next.

## What data sources does lOrdly connect to?

Currently GA4 and Google Search Console. More integrations are planned.

## Is lOrdly open source?

No. Core systems are proprietary.

## Will there be a public API?

Yes. A developer platform and API are planned.
