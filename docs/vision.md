# Vision

## The Problem

Executives and marketing managers at Brazilian SMBs have access to data (GA4, social media, market signals) but lack the capacity to interpret it strategically. They see numbers but can't understand causality. They act on intuition because extracting signal from noise requires expertise they don't have internally.

The result: wasted budgets, stagnant growth, and decisions based on gut feeling — when the real problem is 81% cart abandonment on mobile.

---

## Our Answer

lOrdly is **Decision Intelligence as a Service** — an AI platform that does what a senior strategy consultant does: reads all the data, finds the real problem, quantifies the impact, benchmarks against competitors, and produces an execution plan.

The difference from a dashboard:

| Traditional BI / Analytics | lOrdly |
|---|---|
| Displays metrics | Interprets patterns |
| What happened | Why it happened |
| You analyze | AI analyzes for you |
| Neutral data | Prioritized action |
| Generic insight | Narrative with context |
| Requires analyst | Built for the CEO |

## Why This Works

1. **GA4 is rich but unreadable** — most SMBs have it connected, few understand it deeply
2. **Competitive context is invisible** — businesses don't know what competitors are doing
3. **Executive time is zero** — CEOs won't learn BI tools; they need verdicts, not charts
4. **Brazilian market gap** — no competitor offers narrative intelligence for SMBs in PT-BR

## What Success Looks Like

The user opens the platform and reads, in plain language:

> *"Your mobile conversion rate is 7x lower than desktop. This is costing you R$ 210,000 per month. Competitor A has already solved this. Here is the action plan."*

That is the experience. Not a dashboard. An intelligence.
